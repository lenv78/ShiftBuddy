Section 01

Data Collection and Usage

The App does not collect, use, or share any personal user data. Your privacy is fully protected by design — everything stays on your device.

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other identifying information.
  • No Tracking: We do not track your location, device usage, or any other metrics.

Section 02

Cookies and Web Beacons

The App does not use "cookies" or any other tracking technologies. Since the app does not communicate with a server for data storage, no tracking mechanisms are implemented.

Section 03

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services — such as analytics, advertising networks, or payment processors — that could collect information used to identify you. What happens on your device stays on your device.
